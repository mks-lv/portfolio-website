Hobbys

To be honest, I’m happiest when I’m being creative, even if I don’t see myself as a pro haha.
When I have some free time, I love sitting down at the accordion or other keyboards. For me, it’s less about playing perfectly and more about the joy of making music and just trying things out. It’s a similar story with drawing and painting. These are just ways for me to clear my head without the pressure of creating a masterpiece every time.

A big part of me is also tied to projects that are close to my heart. I’m very active in social justice and dedicated to environmental protection. It’s important to me not to just stand by, but to get involved and advocate for social equality wherever I can.

On the digital side, I love diving into design and tech. Whether I’m designing layouts in the Affinity Suite, building 3D models in Blender, or editing videos, I just love the process of creating things. Sometimes I even get lost in game programming. It’s that mix of technical tinkering and creative chaos that I enjoy the most.
